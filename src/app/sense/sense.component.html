How Does It Work?

How to Use Guide

This tool is based off of ioStux's "Finding your PERFECT Sensitivity" video. It uses the PSA Method to find a sensitivty that requires less warming up and that is suited specifically for you. [1]


First, you need to find your starting or base sensitivity. To do this, you go in-game play with your sensitivity to find out what sensitivity is needed to complete one 360 degree using one full swipe from one edge of the mousepad to the other.

Once that is entered, you will get three numbers a low, reference, and high sensitivity.
